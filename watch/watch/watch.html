Apple Watch

To wear it is to love it.

Get to know Apple Watch.

  • Fitness

    Every move counts.

    Fitness

    Every move counts.

    Close your rings.

    Three rings show all the ways you’re active and help you reach your Move, Exercise and Stand goals every day.

    Works for all your workouts.

    From cycling to HIIT to yoga, you have a healthy number of ways to work out — with accurate tracking of all the metrics you most want to see.

    Hit the ground running.

    Apple Watch has everything you need for running. You can use views like Heart Rate Zones, target specific time goals with Pacer or do interval training with custom workouts.

    Go above, below and beyond.

    Apple Watch is water resistant,1 dust resistant2 and crack resistant. So you can swim, hike and do everything in between without a worry.

  • Health

    Knows you by heart.

    Health

    Knows you by heart.

    Never miss a beat.

    With the ECG app, Apple Watch is capable of generating an ECG that is similar to a single‑lead electrocardiogram.3 And the Heart Rate app alerts you of high and low heart rates, as well as an irregular rhythm.4

    You snooze. You win.

    Get help setting up a bedtime routine and tracking your sleep. You can even see the time you spent in REM, Core and Deep sleep, as well as when you might have woken up.

    The very picture of health.

    Apple Watch works with the Health app on iPhone to give you a better view of important things like your sleep, activity, heart rate, menstrual cycle5 and mental health.

  • Connectivity

    The right call for
    staying in touch.

    Connectivity

    The right call for staying in touch.

    It answers the call. And text.

    Call, text, get notifications and listen to music from almost anywhere. With a cellular plan, you can do it all without your phone while jogging or running an errand.6

    Your wallet. Without the wallet.

    Your wallet. Without the wallet. Use Wallet for your boarding pass, tickets and more.7

    Music and podcasts to your ears.

    Stream over 100 million songs with Apple Music.8 Download podcasts to your watch. Enjoy that new audiobook. And listen to content from your other favourite apps.

  • Safety

    Good help is
    easy to find.

    Safety

    Good help is easy to find.

    Emergency? At your service.

    Apple Watch can detect if you take a hard fall or are in a severe crash and then automatically call for help, share your location and notify your emergency contacts.9

    Find My. What a find.

    The Find My app helps you easily find a friend in a crowded place or know when a family member has arrived home safely. You can also locate your lost or stolen devices.

    Sound the alarm.

    With Siren on Apple Watch Ultra 2, you can quickly alert those around you when you need help. Just press and hold the Action button and wait for the countdown.

  • Apple Watch + iPhone

    Dynamic duo.

    Apple Watch + iPhone

    Dynamic duo.

    Lost and pinged.

    Ping your nearby iPhone to hear where it’s hiding. On Apple Watch Series 9 and Apple Watch Ultra 2, Precision Finding can direct you to your iPhone 15 by showing its approximate distance and direction.10

    Lights. Camera. Remote.

    Set up a group photo on iPhone, then use your watch to see and snap the shot or set a timer. It’s perfect for controlling your camera remotely.

    Turn-by-turn by wrist.

    Maps can provide turn-by-turn directions along with a haptic tap on your wrist when it’s time to turn. Great for when you’re walking on a busy street or biking a new route.

  • Personalisation

    Make it you-nique.

    Personalisation

    Make it you-nique.

    Flaunt your favourite straps.

    Watch straps come in a wide range of colours, styles and materials, so it’s easy to switch up your look to match your mood.

    Dial up the faces.

    You can create an endless number of face combinations on Apple Watch — everything from Astronomy to Nike to Pride to Snoopy. Customise them, choose your complications and even share with friends.

    App Store. Open to possibilities.

    The App Store on Apple Watch gives you access to tens of thousands of apps right on your wrist, like Waterllama and SwingVision. It lets you personalise your watch for whatever you love to do.

  • Adventure Ready

    Go the Ultra mile.

    Adventure Ready

    Go the Ultra mile.

    Strength in metrics.

    The larger screen of Apple Watch Ultra 2 allows you to take in more data while you take on more. So whether you’re running, hiking, biking, diving or exploring, it’s got your number.

    GPS far ahead of the curve.

    Apple Watch Ultra 2 features precision dual-frequency GPS, which makes it the most accurate GPS in a sports watch for running in dense urban environments.11

    Action packed.

    The customisable Action button gives you physical control over a variety of functions. Quickly start or stop a workout, mark a segment, drop a Compass Waypoint or begin a dive.

    Go further. Go longer.

    Apple Watch Ultra 2 gets up to 36 hours of normal use and up to 72 hours with low power settings enabled.12 More than enough to take on a marathon, long-form triathlon or 3-day hike.

  • Family Setup for Apple Watch

    Great for kids who don’t have an iPhone yet.

    Family Setup for Apple Watch

    Great for kids who don’t
    have an iPhone yet.

    A great family connection.

    Kids can enjoy all the fun, connectivity and activity tracking of Apple Watch, even if they don’t have their own iPhone.13

    Has everyone talking.

    Set up and manage watches for your children with your iPhone so they can make calls and send texts with their own phone number and Apple ID. You can even approve who they contact.

    Keep close watch.

    Your kids can share their location on Apple Watch, so you can receive alerts when they arrive at their destinations or get home.
